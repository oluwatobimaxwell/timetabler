NOTE

  • Type of constraints!

    The Lecturer and Student constraints are soft constraints, while General and Religion are user-defined hard constraints. Other hard constraints are in-built, you can see the list of them in Documentation.

  • How system handles constraints!

    This means that the system complete the in-build hard constraints and the user-defined hard constraints first, before trying as much as posible to satisfy the soft constraints.
